<p><p>Polystyrene microspheres are available in two main types: hollow microspheres and solid microspheres. Hollow microspheres are lightweight and have a low thermal conductivity, making them ideal for industries such as construction, aerospace, and automotive for applications like insulation and composites. Solid microspheres, on the other hand, are dense and offer strong mechanical properties, making them suitable for uses in paints, coatings, adhesives, and medical devices. Both types of microspheres offer unique advantages and cater to different industry needs.</p></p>
