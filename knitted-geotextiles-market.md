<p><strong>The Knitted Geotextiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PP</li><li>PET</li><li>PA</li><li>HDPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Knitted geotextiles are commonly categorized based on the material they are made of, such as polypropylene (PP), polyester (PET), polyamide (PA), and high-density polyethylene (HDPE). PP geotextiles are known for their strength and durability, making them suitable for applications requiring high tensile strength. PET geotextiles are resistant to chemicals and UV rays, making them ideal for long-term applications. PA geotextiles offer high resistance to abrasion and provide excellent filtration properties. HDPE geotextiles are typically used in drainage and filtration applications due to their permeability and chemical resistance.</p></p>
