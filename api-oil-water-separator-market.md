There are different types of API oil water separator markets including coalescing plate oil water separators, hydrocyclone oil water separators, and vertical gravity oil water separators. Coalescing plate separators use plates to separate oil from water, hydrocyclone separators use centrifugal force, and vertical gravity separators use gravity to separate the two liquids.
