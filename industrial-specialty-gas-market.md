<p><p>Industrial Specialty Gas market can be segmented into four types based on their properties and applications - Flammable Gas, Oxidizing Gas, Corrosive Gas, and Inert Gas. Flammable gases like hydrogen and methane support combustion, Oxidizing gases like oxygen and chlorine promote combustion, Corrosive gases like ammonia and hydrogen chloride can cause damage on contact, and Inert gases like nitrogen and argon do not react with other substances.
