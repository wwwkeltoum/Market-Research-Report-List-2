<p><p>Synthetic gear oils fall into two main categories: fully synthetic oil and semi synthetic oil. Fully synthetic oil is made entirely of chemically engineered materials, offering the best performance and protection for high-stress applications. On the other hand, semi synthetic oil is a blend of synthetic and conventional oil, offering a balance between performance and cost-effectiveness. Both types provide superior thermal stability, reduced friction, and better engine efficiency compared to traditional mineral oils, making them popular choices for various industries.</p></p>
