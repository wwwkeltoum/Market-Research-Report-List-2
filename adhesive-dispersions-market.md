<p><strong>The Adhesive Dispersions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Styrene/Acrylate Dispersions</li><li>VAE Dispersions</li><li>SBL (Styrene Butadiene Latex)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Adhesive dispersions are categorized into different types based on their composition and properties. Styrene/acrylate dispersions are commonly used for high-performance adhesive applications due to their excellent adhesion properties. VAE dispersions, on the other hand, provide flexibility and water resistance, making them suitable for flexible adhesive products. SBL (Styrene Butadiene Latex) dispersions offer good adhesion to a variety of substrates and are commonly used in construction adhesives and sealants. Each type of dispersion has specific advantages and is used in different applications based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
