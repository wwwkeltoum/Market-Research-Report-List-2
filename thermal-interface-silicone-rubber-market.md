<p><p>Thermal Interface Silicone Rubber Market offers three main types: Sheet Type, Cap Type, and Tube Type. Sheet Type silicone rubber is widely used for heat dissipation in electronic devices as it provides a smooth and thin interface between surfaces. Cap Type silicone rubber is preferred for applications where precise dispensing is required, such as in microelectronics. Tube Type silicone rubber is commonly used for sealing gaps and filling small spaces in electronic components. Each type serves a specific purpose in thermal management and heat transfer applications.</p></p>
