<p><strong>The Electric Water Heaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Storage</li><li>Non-storage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric water heaters can be categorized into two main types: storage and non-storage. </p><p>1. Storage water heaters store and heat water in a tank, ensuring hot water is readily available for use at all times. These heaters are suitable for larger households or commercial buildings where there is a high demand for hot water.</p><p>2. Non-storage water heaters, also known as tankless water heaters, heat water on demand as it passes through the unit. These heaters are more energy-efficient as they only heat water when needed, making them ideal for smaller households or applications with lower hot water demand.</p></p>
