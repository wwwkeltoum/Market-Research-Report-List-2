<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Moldable</li><li>Granules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bioactive materials are classified into three main types: powder, moldable, and granules. Powder bioactive materials are fine particles that can be easily mixed with other substances to form a paste or solution. Moldable bioactive materials have a putty-like consistency, allowing them to be shaped and molded into the desired form. Granules are small, solid particles that can be packed into bone voids or cavities for bone regeneration. Each type of bioactive material offers unique advantages and applications in the healthcare and biomedical industries.</p></p>
