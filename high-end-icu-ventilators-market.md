<p><ul><li>Invasive Ventilation</li><li>Non-invasive Ventilation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High end ICU ventilators are classified into two market types: Invasive Ventilation and Non-invasive Ventilation. </p><p>- Invasive Ventilation requires the insertion of a tube into the patient's airway to assist with breathing. This method is commonly used for patients who cannot breathe on their own.</p><p>- Non-invasive Ventilation, on the other hand, delivers oxygen through a mask or nasal prongs without the need for intubation. This method is often used for patients with respiratory conditions that do not require invasive intervention.</p></p>
