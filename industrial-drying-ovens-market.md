Some common types include rotary drying ovens, which use rotating drums to dry materials, vacuum drying ovens that operate under reduced pressure, conveyor dryers that continuously move materials through the drying process, and gravity convection or forced air convection drying ovens that use natural or forced airflow for drying. Basic double wall drying ovens provide insulation to maintain a consistent temperature. Each type of drying oven offers unique advantages for different applications in industrial settings.</p></p>
