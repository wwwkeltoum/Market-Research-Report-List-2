<p><p>Industrial silica sand can be categorized into three main types based on its particle size: less than 40 mesh, 40-70 mesh, and more than 70 mesh. Less than 40 mesh silica sand is coarser and commonly used in applications where a rough texture is required. 40-70 mesh silica sand is of medium coarseness and is suitable for a wide range of industrial uses. More than 70 mesh silica sand is fine and is typically used in applications that require a very smooth finish or precise filtration.</p></p>
