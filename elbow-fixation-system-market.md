<p><p>Elbow Fixation System market includes External Fixation and Internal Fixation types. External Fixation involves the use of external devices such as pins and wires to stabilize the elbow joint, while Internal Fixation utilizes internal implants like plates and screws to secure the joint. Patients and healthcare providers may choose between these options based on the severity of the elbow injury and individual preferences.</p></p>
