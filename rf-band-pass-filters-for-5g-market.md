<p><p>RF band pass filters are crucial components in 5G networks, allowing specific frequency ranges to pass through while blocking others. Three main types dominating the market are cavity filters, ceramic filters, and BAW filters. Cavity filters use resonant cavities to achieve high selectivity and low insertion loss. Ceramic filters offer compact size and excellent performance for high-frequency applications. BAW filters use piezoelectric materials to provide high-Q and high power handling capabilities, making them ideal for 5G applications requiring high data rates and low latency.</p></p>
