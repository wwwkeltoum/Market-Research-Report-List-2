<p><p>Geotechnical measuring devices are used for monitoring soil and rock conditions in civil engineering projects. Extensometers are used to measure ground settlement and deformation. Piezometers measure pore water pressure. Rain gauges measure precipitation levels. Thermometers monitor temperature fluctuations in the ground. Other devices may include inclinometers, pressure cells, and tiltmeters. These devices provide crucial data for assessing the stability and safety of construction sites, dams, tunnels, and other infrastructure projects.</p></p>
