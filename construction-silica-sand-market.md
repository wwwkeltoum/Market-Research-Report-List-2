<p><p>Construction silica sand is classified into three main types based on particle size: less than 40 mesh, 40-70 mesh, and more than 70 mesh. Less than 40 mesh silica sand is fine-grained and used in applications where a smooth finish is needed. 40-70 mesh silica sand is commonly used in aggregate for concrete and asphalt mixtures. More than 70 mesh silica sand is coarse and used in projects where strength and durability are prioritized, such as in road construction and heavy-duty concrete projects.</p></p>
