<p><p>The video intercom system market consists of three main types: door stations, video intercom masters, and indoor units. Door stations are installed outside a building to allow visitors to communicate with residents inside. Video intercom masters are the central control units that manage communication and access control functions. Indoor units are installed within the building to allow residents to communicate with visitors and grant access. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring seamless and secure communication and access control within a building.</p></p>
