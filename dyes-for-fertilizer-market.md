<p><p>Dyes for fertilizer are available in two main types: powder and liquid. Powder dyes are typically more concentrated and are easier to store and transport. They are often added to dry fertilizers before application. Liquid dyes, on the other hand, are easier to mix with liquid fertilizers and are more evenly distributed. They are also more convenient for use in foliar applications. Both powder and liquid dyes are widely used in the fertilizer industry to help monitor and track the application of fertilizers on crops.</p></p>
