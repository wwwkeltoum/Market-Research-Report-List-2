<p><p>Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) equipment market includes various types such as Metal ALD, Aluminum Oxide ALD, ALD on Polymers, and Catalytic ALD. Metal ALD involves the deposition of thin films of metals on a substrate. Aluminum Oxide ALD is the process of depositing alumina coatings. ALD on Polymers refers to depositing ALD films on polymer substrates. Catalytic ALD involves the use of catalysts to enhance the ALD process. These different types cater to a wide range of industries and applications.</p></p>
