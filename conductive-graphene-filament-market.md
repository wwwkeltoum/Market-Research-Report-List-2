<p><strong>The Conductive Graphene Filament Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Asphalt Based</li><li>Pan Based</li><li>Viscose Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conductive graphene filaments are categorized into three main types based on their manufacturing process and composition: Asphalt Based, Pan Based, and Viscose Based. Asphalt based graphene filaments are derived from asphalt materials, providing a cost-effective option with good conductivity. Pan based filaments are manufactured from polyacrylonitrile, offering high strength and durability. Viscose based filaments are produced from viscose materials, providing flexibility and ease of use. Each type caters to different applications and requirements in the market.</p></p>
