<p><p>Copolymer Coated Aluminium Tape comes in two main market types: Single Sided and Double Sided. Single Sided tapes have the copolymer coating on only one side of the tape, providing a protective barrier for the aluminum surface. On the other hand, Double Sided tapes have the copolymer coating on both sides, offering increased protection and adhesion for applications where both surfaces need to be secured. Both types of tapes are commonly used in industries like construction, automotive, and electrical for various sealing and bonding applications.</p></p>
