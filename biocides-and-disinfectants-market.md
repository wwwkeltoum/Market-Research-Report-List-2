<p><p>Biocides and disinfectants market can be categorized into non-oxidizing chemicals, oxidizing chemicals, and others. Non-oxidizing chemicals work by disrupting the cell membrane or metabolism of microorganisms, while oxidizing chemicals release oxygen radicals to destroy pathogens. The "others" category includes alternative disinfectants such as alcohols, acids, and biguanides. Each type of biocide and disinfectant has its own mechanism of action and effectiveness in eliminating harmful microorganisms, making them essential tools in maintaining cleanliness and hygiene in various industries.</p></p>
