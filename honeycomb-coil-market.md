<p><p>Honeycomb coils are typically categorized into three main types: oscillating coils, deflection coils, and others. Oscillating coils are designed to provide a controlled movement or vibration, often used in applications requiring precise motion control. Deflection coils, on the other hand, are used to bend or distort under a load, helping to distribute stress evenly. Other types of honeycomb coils may have unique functionalities or characteristics that set them apart from these two main categories.</p></p>
