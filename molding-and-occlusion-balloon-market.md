<p><p>Molding and occlusion balloon market types include normal balloon catheters, cutting balloon catheters, scoring balloon catheters, drug eluting balloon catheters, and stent graft balloon catheters. Normal balloon catheters are used for routine dilation procedures, while cutting balloon catheters have small blades to cut through tissue. Scoring balloon catheters have micro blades for precise scoring, drug eluting balloon catheters release medication to prevent restenosis, and stent graft balloon catheters are used in conjunction with stent grafts for endovascular repair. Each type offers specific benefits for different medical procedures.</p></p>
