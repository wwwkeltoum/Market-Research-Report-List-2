<p><p>Eyelash extension products can be categorized into three main market types: vitamin-based, prostaglandin-derived, and peptide-based. Vitamin-based products contain vitamins and nutrients that promote eyelash growth, health, and strength. Prostaglandin-derived products contain active ingredients that stimulate the growth phase of eyelashes, resulting in longer and thicker lashes. Peptide-based products contain amino acids that strengthen and nourish eyelashes, promoting overall lash health. Each market type offers unique benefits for individuals looking to enhance the appearance and health of their eyelashes.</p></p>
