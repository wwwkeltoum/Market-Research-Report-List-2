<p><p>Full-automatic parcel sorting robots require minimal human intervention during the sorting process, as they can fully automate the sorting process from start to finish. These robots are typically more expensive but offer higher efficiency and accuracy in sorting parcels. On the other hand, semi-automatic parcel sorting robots involve some level of human intervention in the sorting process, such as loading and unloading parcels. This type of robot is generally more cost-effective and is suitable for smaller operations with less sorting volume.</p></p>
