<p><p>PVC conduit pipes are commonly grouped into two types in the market: Flexible Type and Rigid Type. Flexible PVC conduit pipes are known for their bendability, making them ideal for installations in tight or irregular spaces. On the other hand, Rigid PVC conduit pipes are more sturdy and provide added protection for wiring in areas where impact or crushing may occur. Both types offer advantages depending on the application, with a variety of sizes and specifications available to suit different needs.</p></p>
