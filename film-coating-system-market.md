<p><ul><li>Aqueous Film Coating</li><li>Non Aqueous Film Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Film coating systems are used in the pharmaceutical industry to provide a protective and aesthetic coating on tablets and pills. Aqueous film coating involves the use of water-based solutions, offering advantages such as environmental friendliness and ease of use. Non-aqueous film coating, on the other hand, uses solvents other than water for the coating process. This type of coating is preferred in situations where water may cause product degradation or where specific properties such as controlled release are required.</p></p>
