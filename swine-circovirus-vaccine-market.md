<p><p>Swine Circovirus Vaccine market includes two main types: Genetic Engineering Vaccine and Killed Vaccines. Genetic Engineering Vaccines are produced using modern biotechnology techniques to insert genetic material from the virus into a harmless carrier, stimulating immune response in pigs. Killed Vaccines are made by inactivating the virus with chemicals, allowing the immune system to recognize and attack the virus if exposed in the future.
