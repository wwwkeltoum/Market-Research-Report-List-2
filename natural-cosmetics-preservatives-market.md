<p><p>Natural cosmetics preservatives market can be categorized into various types such as lipids, acids, alcohols, and others. Lipid-based preservatives are derived from natural oils and fats and are effective in preventing microbial growth. Acid-based preservatives like citric acid and sorbic acid lower the pH level of the product, hindering bacterial growth. Alcohol-based preservatives like ethanol and propanediol also act as antimicrobial agents. Other natural preservatives include plant extracts and essential oils which have antimicrobial properties.</p></p>
