<p><ul><li>CSF Shunts</li><li>CSF Drainage Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) Management Devices Market encompasses two main types of devices: CSF Shunts and CSF Drainage Systems. CSF shunts are implantable devices used to divert excess fluid away from the brain to another part of the body where it can be absorbed. CSF Drainage Systems, on the other hand, are external devices used to drain excess fluid from the brain via a catheter. Both devices play a crucial role in managing conditions such as hydrocephalus and traumatic brain injuries.</p></p>
