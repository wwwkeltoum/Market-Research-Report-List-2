<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Liquid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Seed colorants are available in two main forms - powder and liquid. Powder colorants are typically dry, finely ground particles that are mixed with seeds using equipment such as tumblers or mixers. Liquid colorants, on the other hand, are concentrated solutions that can be applied directly to seeds using spray equipment. Both types of colorants are used to enhance the visual appearance of seeds, provide differentiation in the marketplace, and improve seed handling efficiency. The market for seed colorants is driven by factors such as increasing demand for high-quality seeds and growing focus on sustainable agriculture practices.</p></p>
