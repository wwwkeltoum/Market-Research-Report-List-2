<p><p>Organic Plant Growth Regulators market consists of three main types: Auxins, Cytokinins, and Gibberellins. Auxins promote cell elongation and root growth, making them ideal for rooting hormone products. Cytokinins stimulate cell division and growth in plants, leading to improved flowering and fruit development. Gibberellins help in elongating stem and increasing seed germination rates. Each type of growth regulator plays a crucial role in promoting healthier plant growth and can be used effectively in agricultural practices to improve crop yields and overall plant health.</p></p>
