<p><p>PET Monofilament market types are categorized based on their diameter size. Monofilaments with a diameter of less than 0.1 mm are used for applications requiring very fine and delicate threads. Monofilaments with a diameter of 0.1-0.8 mm are commonly used in various industrial applications such as filtration, textiles, and fishing lines. Monofilaments with a diameter of 0.81-1.5 mm are used for more heavy-duty applications like brush bristles, ropes, and cables. Monofilaments with a diameter larger than 1.5 mm are typically used in construction, agriculture, and geotextiles due to their strength and durability.</p></p>
