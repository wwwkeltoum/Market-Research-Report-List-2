<p><p>Fluororubber, also known as FKM, FSR, and FFKM, is a type of synthetic rubber that offers high resistance to heat, chemicals, and oil. FKM is the most common type of fluororubber, known for its excellent chemical resistance. FSR is a specialty fluororubber that provides superior resistance to fuels and solvents. FFKM is a premium grade fluororubber with the highest level of resistance to extreme temperatures and harsh chemicals. These different types cater to a wide range of industries where extreme conditions are present.</p></p>
