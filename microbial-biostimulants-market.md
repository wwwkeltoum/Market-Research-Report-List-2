<p><p>Microbial biostimulants are classified into two main types: natural biostimulants and synthetic biostimulants. Natural biostimulants are derived from organic sources such as plant extracts, seaweed, and beneficial microorganisms. They enhance plant growth and development by stimulating natural processes in the plant. Synthetic biostimulants, on the other hand, are chemically synthesized compounds that mimic the effects of natural biostimulants. Both types of biostimulants play a crucial role in promoting plant growth, improving nutrient uptake, and boosting crop yields in agriculture.</p></p>
