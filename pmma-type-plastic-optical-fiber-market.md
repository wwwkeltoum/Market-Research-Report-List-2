<p><strong>The PMMA Type Plastic Optical Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Step Index Profile</li><li>Graded Index Profile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PMMA Type Plastic Optical Fiber Market Types include Step Index Profile and Graded Index Profile. Step Index Profile fibers have a uniform refractive index within the core, leading to straight light transmission paths. Graded Index Profile fibers have a core with a decreasing refractive index towards the cladding, allowing for curved light paths and reduced modal dispersion. Both types are commonly used in communication and lighting applications due to their flexibility, durability, and high transmission speeds.</p></p>
