<p><strong>The Inductive Linear Displacement Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Variable Reluctance Sensor</li><li>Differential Transformer Sensor</li><li>Eddy Current Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inductive linear displacement sensors are available in three main types. Variable reluctance sensors utilize a movable core and coil to measure distance changes. Differential transformer sensors rely on variations in voltage induced by a moving core within a coil. Eddy current sensors use electromagnetic induction to detect displacements. These sensors are widely used in various industries for accurate and precise measurements of linear displacements, providing reliable and efficient solutions for monitoring and control applications.</p></p>
