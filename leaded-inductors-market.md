<p><strong>The Leaded Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial</li><li>Radial</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Leaded inductors are classified into three main market types: axial, radial, and through hole. Axial inductors have their leads extending from opposite ends of the component, making them suitable for applications where space is limited. Radial inductors have their leads emerging from one side of the component, making them easy to mount on circuit boards. Through hole inductors have their leads inserted through holes on the board, providing stable and reliable connections. Each market type caters to different application requirements.</p></p>
