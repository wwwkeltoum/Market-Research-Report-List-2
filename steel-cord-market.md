<p><strong>The Steel Cord Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Tensile (NT)</li><li>High Tensile (HT)</li><li>Super Tensile (ST)</li><li>Ultra Tensile (UT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel cord is classified into different types based on their tensile strength. Normal Tensile (NT) steel cords have the lowest tensile strength, followed by High Tensile (HT), Super Tensile (ST), and Ultra Tensile (UT) cords which have increasingly higher tensile strength levels. NT cords are suitable for general applications, while HT cords are used for heavier loads. ST and UT cords are designed for extreme conditions where the highest tensile strength is required, such as in mining or construction industries.</p></p>
