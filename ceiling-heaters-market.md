<p><p>Ceiling heaters come in various types, with two popular options being panel heaters and cassette heaters. Panel heaters are flat and slim units that are typically mounted on the ceiling and provide even heat distribution throughout a room. On the other hand, cassette heaters are recessed into the ceiling and blend seamlessly into the ceiling design. Both types of heaters are efficient in heating spaces and are commonly used in commercial buildings, offices, and residential spaces.</p></p>
