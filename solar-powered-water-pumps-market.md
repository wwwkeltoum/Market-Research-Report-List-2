<p><p>Solar-powered water pumps are becoming increasingly popular due to their sustainability and efficiency. There are two main types of solar-powered water pumps on the market: Direct Current (DC) pumps and Alternate Current (AC) pumps. DC pumps are more commonly used for smaller applications such as irrigation and livestock water supply, while AC pumps are better suited for larger-scale applications such as municipal water supply and industrial use. Both types of pumps harness the power of sunlight to operate, making them environmentally friendly and cost-effective solutions for water pumping needs.</p></p>
