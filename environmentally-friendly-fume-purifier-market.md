<p><p>The Environmentally Friendly Fume Purifier Market includes various types such as Mechanical Separation, Catalytic Combustion, Activated Carbon Adsorption, Fabric Filter, Wet Processing, Photocatalytic, and Electrostatic Treatment. Mechanical Separation uses physical barriers to separate pollutants from fumes. Catalytic Combustion involves using a catalyst to convert pollutants into less harmful substances. Activated Carbon Adsorption traps pollutants in a porous material. Fabric Filter captures pollutants with filter media. Wet Processing involves scrubbing pollutants with water. Photocatalytic and Electrostatic Treatment use advanced technologies to remove pollutants from fumes.</p></p>
