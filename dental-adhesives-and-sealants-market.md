<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dental Adhesives and Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-etching Adhesive</li><li>Selective-etching Adhesive</li><li>Total-etching Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental adhesives and sealants market offers three main types: self-etching adhesive, selective-etching adhesive, and total-etching adhesive. Self-etching adhesives eliminate the need for separate etching steps by simultaneously etching and priming the tooth surface. Selective-etching adhesives involve selectively etching the enamel to improve bonding. Total-etching adhesives involve the separate application of etchant and primer to achieve optimal bonding strength. These variations cater to different dental procedures and preferences, providing dentists with options to meet specific patient needs.</p></p>
