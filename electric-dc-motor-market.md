<p><strong>The Electric DC Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brushed</li><li>Brushless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric DC motors are classified into two main types: brushed and brushless. Brushed motors have brushes that make physical contact with the commutator, while brushless motors do not have brushes and instead rely on electronic commutation. Brushed motors are typically less expensive and easier to control, making them suitable for applications where cost is a primary concern. Brushless motors, on the other hand, offer higher efficiency, longer lifespan, and reduced maintenance requirements, making them ideal for more demanding applications that require precision and reliability.</p></p>
