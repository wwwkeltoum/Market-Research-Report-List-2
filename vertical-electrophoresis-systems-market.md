<p><p>Vertical Electrophoresis Systems are typically used for separating nucleic acids or proteins based on size, charge, and other properties. Common types of vertical electrophoresis systems include Agarose Gel Electrophoresis System and Polyacrylamide Gel Electrophoresis System. Agarose gel systems are often used for separating larger DNA fragments, while polyacrylamide gel systems are preferred for smaller DNA fragments or proteins. Both systems operate on the principle of applying an electric field to move molecules through a gel matrix for separation and analysis.</p></p>
