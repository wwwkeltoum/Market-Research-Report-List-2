<p><ul><li>Integrated Heat Meter</li><li>Split Heat Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic heat meters are divided into two main types: integrated heat meters and split heat meters. Integrated heat meters combine the ultrasonic flow sensor and the calculator in a single device, making them easy to install and use. Split heat meters have the flow sensor and calculator separated into two units connected by a cable, allowing for more flexibility in installation. Both types of heat meters use ultrasonic technology to accurately measure heat energy consumption in various heating systems.</p></p>
