<p><p>Dissolved Airfloatation Unit is available in two main types, Open Tank and Plate Pack. Open tank units utilize a large tank where water is treated with air bubbles to separate solid particles. Plate pack units consist of a series of inclined plates that create a channel for the treated water to flow through, allowing for efficient separation of solids. Both types of DAF units are commonly used in industries such as wastewater treatment, oil and gas, and mining for their effectiveness in removing contaminants from water.</p></p>
