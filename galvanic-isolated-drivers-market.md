<p><p>Galvanic Isolated Drivers are essential components in the control of power semiconductors such as IGBTs and MOSFETs. IGBT Drivers are specifically designed for Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors, providing the necessary high voltage and high current drive capabilities. On the other hand, MOSFET Drivers are tailored for Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors, offering precise control over switching operations. Both types of drivers offer galvanic isolation to ensure safety and reliability in high-power applications, making them crucial in various industries such as automotive, industrial, and renewable energy.</p></p>
