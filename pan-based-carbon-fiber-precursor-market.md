<p><p>The PAN-based Carbon Fiber Precursor market can be categorized into two types based on the size of the tow - small tow and big tow. Small tow refers to carbon fiber precursors with a lower number of filaments twisted together, resulting in a finer and more flexible product suitable for applications requiring high strength and stiffness. On the other hand, big tow consists of carbon fiber precursors with a higher number of filaments twisted together, producing a thicker and stiffer product ideal for applications requiring high impact resistance and durability.</p></p>
