<p><p>Smart irrigation market can be classified into two main types based on the technology used: weather-based controller systems and sensor-based controller systems. Weather-based systems use real-time weather data to adjust irrigation schedules accordingly, reducing water wastage and optimizing plant growth. Sensor-based systems use soil moisture sensors, weather stations, and other sensors to monitor environmental conditions and plant needs, adjusting irrigation schedules as needed. Both systems aim to improve water efficiency and reduce water usage in agricultural and landscaping applications.</p></p>
