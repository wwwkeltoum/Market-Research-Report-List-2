<p><ul><li>3-point Bend</li><li>4-point Bend</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bend fixtures are used in various markets for material testing applications. The 3-point bend market typically involves a loading system with two supports and a single load applied in the center, making it suitable for testing materials that are brittle or have low strength. On the other hand, the 4-point bend market uses a loading system with two supports and two loads applied at equal distances from the center, making it ideal for testing materials that are more ductile or have higher strength. Both market types offer specific advantages depending on the material being tested.</p></p>
