<p><p>Organic LED (OLED) technology is divided into two main types: Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) and Active Matrix OLED (AMOLED). PMOLEDs are simpler and less expensive, suitable for small displays and simple graphical interfaces. On the other hand, AMOLEDs are more advanced, offering faster response times and higher resolutions, making them ideal for larger displays such as smartphones and TVs. Both types of OLED have gained popularity in the market due to their energy efficiency, superior picture quality, and flexibility in design.</p></p>
