<p><strong>The Polyester Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyester Staple Fiber (PSF)</li><li>Polyester Filament Yarn (PFY)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyester Staple Fiber (PSF) is a type of polyester fiber that is made by cutting polyester filament yarn into shorter pieces. PSF is widely used in the production of textiles, non-woven fabrics, and filling materials for various products. On the other hand, Polyester Filament Yarn (PFY) is a continuous length of polyester fibers that are twisted together to form a single strand. PFY is commonly used in the manufacturing of fabrics, clothing, and industrial products. Both PSF and PFY play a crucial role in the polyester fiber market.</p></p>
