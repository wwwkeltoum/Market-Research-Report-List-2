<p><p>Mining rubber market types include mill liners, slurry pumps and liners, screen media, and cyclone mills and liners. Mill liners are used to protect the mill shell from wear and tear, while slurry pumps and liners are crucial for transporting abrasive slurries. Screen media is essential for separating different particle sizes, and cyclone mills and liners are designed to handle high-velocity abrasive materials. These rubber products play a key role in ensuring efficient and reliable operations in mining applications.</p></p>
