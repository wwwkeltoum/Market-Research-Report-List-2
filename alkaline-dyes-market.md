<p><p>Alkaline dyes are classified into two types based on their physical form: liquid and powder. Liquid alkaline dyes are pre-dissolved in a liquid solvent, making them easy to use and suitable for applications where precise dosing is required. Powder alkaline dyes, on the other hand, are in a solid state and need to be dissolved in a liquid before use. They are typically more concentrated than liquid dyes and are preferred for bulk dyeing processes. Both types of alkaline dyes offer a wide range of vibrant colors and excellent colorfastness.</p></p>
