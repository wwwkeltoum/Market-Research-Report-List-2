<p><strong>The Etching Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Etching Agent</li><li>Dry Etching Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Etching agents are used in the semiconductor industry for the process of removing layers of material from a surface. There are two main types of etching agents: wet etching agents and dry etching agents. Wet etching agents involve immersing the substrate in a liquid chemical solution to dissolve the material, while dry etching agents use plasma or vapor-phase etchants to remove material through a physical or chemical reaction. Both types of etching agents play a crucial role in the fabrication of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
