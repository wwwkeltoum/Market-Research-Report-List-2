<p><p>Complex Phase Steel (CP Steel) is a high-strength, low-alloy steel that is commonly used in various industries due to its excellent formability and weldability. The two main types of CP Steel markets are Cold-rolled CP Steel and Hot-rolled CP Steel. Cold-rolled CP Steel is processed at a lower temperature, resulting in a smoother surface finish and higher strength, making it ideal for applications requiring precision and durability. In contrast, Hot-rolled CP Steel is processed at a higher temperature, offering better formability and workability, making it suitable for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
