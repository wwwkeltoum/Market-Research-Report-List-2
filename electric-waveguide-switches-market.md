<p><strong>The Electric Waveguide Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rectangular Waveguide Switch</li><li>Double Ridge Waveguide Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric waveguide switches are used in microwave systems for routing signals. Rectangular waveguide switches are widely used for their simple design and low cost, making them suitable for various applications. Double ridge waveguide switches can handle higher power levels and offer better isolation compared to rectangular waveguide switches. They are commonly used in high-power microwave systems where signal integrity and performance are crucial. Both types of switches play a crucial role in ensuring efficient signal routing and transmission in microwave systems.</p></p>
