<p><p>Radiotherapy Equipment Market consists of three main types: External Beam Radiotherapy Equipment, Internal Beam Radiotherapy Equipment, and Systemic Radiotherapy Equipment. </p><p>External Beam Radiotherapy Equipment delivers radiation from outside the body to target cancer cells. Internal Beam Radiotherapy Equipment involves placing a radiation source inside the body near the tumor. Systemic Radiotherapy Equipment administers radiation through the bloodstream to reach cancer cells throughout the body. Each type of equipment plays a crucial role in the treatment of cancer patients, offering different methods of delivering radiation therapy.</p></p>
