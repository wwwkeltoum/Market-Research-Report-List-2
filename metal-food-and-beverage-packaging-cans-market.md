<p><p>Metal food and beverage packaging cans come in two main types: two-piece cans and three-piece cans. Two-piece cans are made from a single piece of metal formed into a can body and bottom. They are typically used for beverages and are more cost-effective to produce. Three-piece cans, on the other hand, consist of a separate lid, body, and bottom sealed together. They are more commonly used for food products and provide a higher level of protection and durability.</p></p>
