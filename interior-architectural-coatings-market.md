<p><p>Interior Architectural Coatings market includes various types such as Acrylic, Alkyd, Epoxy, Polyurethane, and Others. Acrylic coatings are water-based, fast-drying, and environmentally friendly. Alkyd coatings are oil-based and provide a hard finish. Epoxy coatings are known for their durability and resistance to chemicals. Polyurethane coatings are known for their high abrasion resistance and UV protection. Other coatings may include specialty formulas such as elastomeric or intumescent coatings. Each type offers unique properties and benefits for interior architectural applications.</p></p>
