<p><p>Immunochromatographic Assay Kit market includes various types such as Sandwich Assay, Competitive Assay, and Multiplex Detection Assay. In Sandwich Assay, the target molecule is captured by two antibodies for detection. Competitive Assay involves competition between target molecule and labeled molecule for binding to specific antibody. Multiplex Detection Assay allows the simultaneous detection of multiple analytes in a single sample. Each of these types cater to different needs and provide specific advantages in terms of sensitivity, specificity, and ease of use in diagnostic testing.</p></p>
