<p><p>Viscosity Bone Cement Market is categorized into three types based on their viscosity levels - Low Viscosity Bone Cement, Medium Viscosity Bone Cement, and High Viscosity Bone Cement. Low viscosity bone cement has a thinner consistency and is easier to inject, making it suitable for minimally invasive procedures. Medium viscosity bone cement offers a balance between flowability and stability, making it versatile for different surgical techniques. High viscosity bone cement is thicker and provides excellent support and fixation, ideal for complex orthopedic surgeries.</p></p>
