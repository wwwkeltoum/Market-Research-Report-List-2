Companies are focusing on developing capacitors with higher capacitance values, lower ESR (equivalent series resistance), and better temperature stability to meet the evolving demands of modern electronic devices.</p><p>Moreover, the growing adoption of embedded capacitors in IoT devices, power electronics, and renewable energy systems is also driving the market growth.
