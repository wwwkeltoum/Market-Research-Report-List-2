<p><p>Industrial fire sprinklers are crucial for fire protection in commercial and industrial settings. There are three main types of industrial fire sprinklers: dry sprinkler, wet sprinkler, and deluge sprinkler. </p><p>Dry sprinklers use air pressure to keep water out of the pipes until the sprinklers are activated. Wet sprinklers have water in the pipes at all times and release water immediately when heat is detected. Deluge sprinklers release a large volume of water simultaneously over a wide area when triggered by smoke or heat.</p></p>
