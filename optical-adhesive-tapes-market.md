<p><p>Optical adhesive tapes are used in various applications such as bonding, sealing, and mounting in industries like electronics, automotive, and medical. Acrylic optical adhesive tapes offer strong adhesion and high temperature resistance, making them suitable for demanding applications. Both types of tapes are used in applications that require optical clarity and precise bonding.</p></p>
