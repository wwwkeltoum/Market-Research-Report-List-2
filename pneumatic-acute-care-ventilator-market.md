<p><p>Pneumatic Acute Care Ventilator market consists of two main types: non-invasive ventilators and invasive ventilators. Non-invasive ventilators deliver oxygen to the lungs without the need for intubation, making them suitable for patients with mild to moderate respiratory issues. Invasive ventilators, on the other hand, require intubation and deliver oxygen directly into the lungs, making them suitable for patients with severe respiratory issues or those in critical condition. Both types play a crucial role in providing respiratory support in acute care settings.</p></p>
