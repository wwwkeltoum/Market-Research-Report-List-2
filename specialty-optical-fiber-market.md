<p><strong>The Specialty Optical Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multimode PM Fiber</li><li>Single-Mode PM Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Specialty optical fiber market comprises multimode PM fiber and single-mode PM fiber. Multimode PM fiber allows multiple modes of light to travel through it, making it suitable for short-distance applications. Single-mode PM fiber, on the other hand, only allows a single mode of light to travel through it, making it ideal for long-distance communication. These different types cater to various industry needs, offering a wide range of possibilities for optical fiber applications.</p></p>
