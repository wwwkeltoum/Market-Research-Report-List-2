Shell type heat exchangers consist of a shell with tube bundles that allow for the transfer of heat. Tube type heat exchangers have tubes through which fluid flows to transfer heat. The "others" category includes different designs such as plate type and fin type heat exchangers. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the heating or cooling application.</p></p>
