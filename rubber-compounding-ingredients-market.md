<p><p>Rubber compounding ingredients market can be divided into various types based on their functions. Vulcanizing agents are used to crosslink rubber molecules for improved strength. Protective agents enhance rubber's resistance to aging and weathering. Reinforcing agents like carbon black improve mechanical properties. Bonding agents improve adhesion between rubber and other materials. Other additives like plasticizers and fillers serve various purposes in rubber compounding. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing the performance and durability of rubber products.</p></p>
