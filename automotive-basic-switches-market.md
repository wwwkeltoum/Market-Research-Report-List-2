<p><p>Automotive basic switches can be categorized into three main types: Miniature type, Subminiature type, and Ultra Subminiature type. Miniature type switches are slightly larger and commonly used in vehicles for various functions. Subminiature type switches are smaller and more compact, suitable for limited space applications. Ultra Subminiature type switches are the smallest and most space-efficient, ideal for modern vehicles where space is limited. Each type of switch offers different size and functionality options to meet the specific needs of automotive applications.</p></p>
