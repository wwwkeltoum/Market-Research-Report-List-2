<p><p>Beam splitters are optical devices that divide a light beam into two or more separate beams. There are three main types of beam splitters in the market: plate type, pellicle type, and cube type. Plate type beam splitters consist of a thin glass plate with a partially reflective coating, allowing part of the light to pass through and part to be reflected. Pellicle type beam splitters use a thin film of transparent material to divide the light beam. Cube type beam splitters utilize a prism or cube-shaped design to separate the light beam.</p></p>
