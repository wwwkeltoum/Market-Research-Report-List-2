<p><strong>The Coated Gloves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Glove Coated</li><li>Palm and Fingers</li><li>Fingertips Only</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coated gloves are categorized into three types based on the extent of coverage: full glove coated, palm and fingers coated, and fingertips only coated. Full glove coated gloves provide protection throughout the entire glove, including the palm, fingers, and back of the hand. Palm and fingers coated gloves have coating only on the palm and fingers, leaving the back of the hand exposed. Fingertips only coated gloves have coating solely on the fingertips for added dexterity and grip. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
