<p><ul><li>Invariant Inductor</li><li>Variable Inductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molded inductors in the market can be classified into two types: invariant inductors and variable inductors. Invariant inductors have a fixed inductance value and are used in applications where consistent inductance is required. On the other hand, variable inductors allow for adjustable inductance levels, making them suitable for applications where varying inductance levels are necessary. Both types of molded inductors play crucial roles in electronic circuits by controlling the flow of current and enhancing efficiency.</p></p>
