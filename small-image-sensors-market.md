<p><ul><li>Vidicon Image Sensor</li><li>Solid State Image Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vidicon Image Sensors are a type of small image sensor that use a photoconductive material to produce an electronic signal in response to light. They were commonly used in older analog camera systems. Solid State Image Sensors, on the other hand, are a more modern type of small image sensor that use a semiconductor to convert light into electronic signals. They are widely used in digital cameras and smartphones today. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in capturing and processing images in various electronic devices.</p></p>
