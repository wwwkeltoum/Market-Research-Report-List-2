<p><p>Modal fiber is a type of cellulose fiber made from beech trees. It is known for its softness, durability, and moisture-wicking properties, making it a popular choice for clothing and textiles. Modal fiber is often used in clothing items such as underwear, pajamas, shirts, and dresses.</p><p>The modal fiber market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period. Modal blends offer improved properties such as increased strength and elasticity, as well as enhanced dyeability and color retention. This has led to a growing preference for modal blend fabrics in various apparel and textile applications. Overall, the modal fiber market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years due to the strong demand for sustainable and high-quality fibers in the textile industry.</p></p>
