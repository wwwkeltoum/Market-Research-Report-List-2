<p><p>The Axial Bone Densitometer market consists of two main types: DEXA (Dual Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) Axial Bone Densitometer and QCT (Quantitative Computed Tomography) Axial Bone Densitometer. DEXA measures bone mineral density and is commonly used for diagnosing osteoporosis. QCT, on the other hand, provides a more detailed measurement of bone density by using a CT scanner. Both types of densitometers play a vital role in assessing a patient's risk of fractures and monitoring the effectiveness of treatment for osteoporosis.</p></p>
