<p><strong>The Hip Scopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pincer Type</li><li>Cam Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hip scopes are devices used to perform minimally invasive surgeries on the hip joint. There are two main types of hip scopes on the market: Pincer type and Cam type. Pincer type scopes are designed to address issues with hip impingement caused by excess bone growth on the acetabulum. Cam type scopes, on the other hand, target impingement caused by abnormal bone growth on the femoral head. Both types of scopes allow surgeons to access and treat hip joint issues with less tissue damage and faster recovery times for patients.</p></p>
