<p><p>Ferromagnetic fixed inductors are available in two main types in the market: multi-layer type and wire wound type. </p><p>Multi-layer type inductors are made up of multiple layers of coils wound around a core, providing high inductance values in a compact size. </p><p>Wire wound type inductors consist of a single coil wound around a core, offering high current-carrying capacity and low DC resistance. </p><p>Both types are widely used in various electronic devices and applications for their efficient energy storage and filtering capabilities.</p></p>
