<p><ul><li>Rock Salt</li><li>Solar Salt</li><li>Evap Salt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Packaged salt market types include rock salt, solar salt, and evap salt. Rock salt is mined from underground salt deposits and is known for its purity and natural flavor. Solar salt is obtained by evaporating seawater or saltwater lakes under the sun, resulting in large crystals perfect for grinding or coarse salt. Evap salt is produced through the mechanical evaporation of brine in commercial processing plants, creating fine salt crystals suitable for a variety of culinary and industrial uses.</p></p>
