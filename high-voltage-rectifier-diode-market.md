<p><p>High Voltage Rectifier Diodes are classified into two main types: Uni-polar and Bi-polar. Uni-polar High Voltage Rectifier Diodes conduct current in only one direction, making them suitable for applications where current flow is unidirectional. Bi-polar High Voltage Rectifier Diodes, on the other hand, allow current to flow in both directions, making them ideal for applications requiring bidirectional current flow. Both types of diodes play a crucial role in converting alternating current into direct current in high voltage applications.</p></p>
