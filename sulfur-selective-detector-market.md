<p><strong>The Sulfur Selective Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfur Chemiluminescence Detector（SCD）</li><li>Flame Photometric Detector (FPD)</li><li>Mass Spectrometry Detector (MSD)</li><li>Atomic Emission Detection (AED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sulfur Selective Detector market includes various types of detectors such as Sulfur Chemiluminescence Detector (SCD), Flame Photometric Detector (FPD), Mass Spectrometry Detector (MSD), and Atomic Emission Detection (AED). SCD measures sulfur compounds based on their chemiluminescence properties, FPD detects sulfur-containing compounds by their emission of light when burned in a flame, MSD identifies sulfur compounds based on their mass-to-charge ratio, and AED detects sulfur by measuring the light emitted when atoms are excited. Each type of detector has its specific advantages and applications in sulfur analysis.</p></p>
