<p><p>Semi-rigid foams can be classified into two types based on their elastic modulus. Low elastic modulus foams have a softer and more flexible structure, making them suitable for applications where cushioning or vibration dampening is required. On the other hand, high elastic modulus foams are more rigid and sturdy, making them ideal for applications that require structural support or insulation. Each type of foam offers unique properties and benefits, catering to different needs and preferences in various industries.</p></p>
