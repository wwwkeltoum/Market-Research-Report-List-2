<p><ul><li>Cement-based Putty</li><li>Gypsum-based Putty</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interior Wall Putty Powders are primarily classified into two types - Cement-based Putty and Gypsum-based Putty. Cement-based putty is composed of cement, polymers, and additives, providing a strong bonding layer for paint application. On the other hand, Gypsum-based Putty is made from gypsum and other additives, offering a smooth finish to walls. Both types of putty are used for filling imperfections on interior walls, providing a smooth surface for painting and enhancing the overall aesthetic appeal of the walls.</p></p>
