<p><strong>The Textured Pea Protein Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isolated Textured Pea Protein</li><li>Concentrated Textured Pea Protein</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Isolated Textured Pea Protein is a highly refined form of pea protein that has had most of its non-protein components removed, resulting in a protein content of around 90% or higher. On the other hand, Concentrated Textured Pea Protein contains a lower protein content of around 50-70%, as it is less processed and retains more of the pea's original nutrients. Both types of Textured Pea Protein are used as plant-based alternatives to meat in various food products due to their high protein content and texture similar to meat.</p></p>
