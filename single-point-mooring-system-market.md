<p><strong>The Single Point Mooring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drag Embedment Anchors (DEA)</li><li>Vertical Load Anchors (VLA)</li><li>Suction Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single point mooring systems are used to moor vessels in offshore locations, enabling efficient transfer of oil and gas. Three main types of anchors used in these systems are Drag Embedment Anchors (DEA), Vertical Load Anchors (VLA), and Suction Anchors. DEA anchors are buried into the seabed to provide holding power, VLA anchors use vertical tension to secure the mooring system, and Suction Anchors utilize suction to create a strong hold. Each type of anchor offers unique advantages depending on specific project requirements.</p></p>
