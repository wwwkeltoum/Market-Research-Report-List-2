<p><ul><li>Refracting Telescope</li><li>Reflecting Telescope</li><li>Catadioptric Telescope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Telescope market is diverse with three main types: Refracting, Reflecting, and Catadioptric telescopes. Refracting telescopes use lenses to gather and focus light, providing sharp images. Reflecting telescopes use mirrors to gather and focus light, usually offering larger apertures and better light-gathering capabilities. Catadioptric telescopes combine both lenses and mirrors, allowing for compact designs with good image quality. Each type has its advantages and is tailored to different needs, from amateur stargazing to professional astronomy.</p></p>
