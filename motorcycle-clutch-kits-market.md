<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Manual clutch kits require the rider to manually engage and disengage the clutch lever in order to shift gears. This type of clutch is more commonly found on traditional motorcycles and offers more control over gear changes. On the other hand, semi-automatic clutch kits feature a combination of manual and automatic functionality, allowing for easier shifting without the need for constant clutch lever manipulation. This type of clutch is often found on newer motorcycles and appeals to riders looking for a more user-friendly riding experience.</p></p>
