<p><p>The ADAS heaters market can be categorized into two types based on the technology used: Printed Electronics and CNT Film Heaters. Printed electronics utilize conductive ink to create flexible and lightweight heaters for ADAS applications. On the other hand, CNT film heaters utilize carbon nanotube films to generate heat efficiently and evenly. Both types of heaters offer advanced heating solutions for ADAS systems, providing improved performance and reliability in various automotive applications.</p></p>
