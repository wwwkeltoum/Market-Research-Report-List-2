<p><p>Manioc powder market can be broadly categorized into two types: sweet manioc powder and bitter manioc powder. Sweet manioc powder is derived from the sweet variety of the manioc root and is commonly used for baking and cooking purposes due to its neutral taste. On the other hand, bitter manioc powder comes from the bitter variety of the root and requires special processing techniques to remove the toxic compounds. Bitter manioc powder is mainly used in traditional dishes in certain cultures and is not typically consumed in its raw form.</p></p>
