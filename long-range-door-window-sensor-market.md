<p><p>Long Range Door Window Sensors are available in two main market types: Wired and Wireless. Wired Long Range Door Window Sensors require a physical connection to a central monitoring system through cables, providing a stable and reliable connection but requiring installation effort. Wireless Long Range Door Window Sensors, on the other hand, operate without cables, making installation easier and more flexible. They communicate with the central monitoring system using wireless technologies such as Wi-Fi or radio frequency, offering convenient and hassle-free monitoring.</p></p>
