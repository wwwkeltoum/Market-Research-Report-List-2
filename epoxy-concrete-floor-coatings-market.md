<p><p>Epoxy concrete floor coatings come in three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and 100% solids. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly and easy to clean up, making them a popular choice for residential use. Solvent-based coatings are more durable and resistant to chemicals, making them ideal for industrial applications. 100% solids coatings offer the highest level of durability and protection against wear and tear, making them suitable for high-traffic areas. Each type of coating offers different benefits and is suited to different environments.</p></p>
