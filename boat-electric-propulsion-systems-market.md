<p><ul><li>Shaftline Propulsion</li><li>Pod Propulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boat electric propulsion systems come in two main types - shaftline propulsion and pod propulsion. </p><p>Shaftline propulsion involves an electric motor that is connected to the propeller via a driveshaft. This type of system is commonly found in traditional boats and provides efficient propulsion.</p><p>Pod propulsion, on the other hand, features electric motors housed in pods that are mounted directly to the hull of the boat. This design offers greater maneuverability and efficiency, making it popular in modern boat designs.</p></p>
