<p><ul><li>Fluoropolymer</li><li>Non-fluoropolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water and oil repellents are used to protect surfaces from liquids or stains. Fluoropolymer repellents are chemical compounds containing fluorine atoms that create a strong barrier against water and oil. They are highly effective but may have potential environmental concerns. Non-fluoropolymer repellents are alternative formulations that are more environmentally friendly, using silicones or wax-based compounds to repel liquids. While less efficient than fluoropolymer repellents, they are becoming increasingly popular due to growing concerns about the environmental impact of fluorine-containing compounds.</p></p>
