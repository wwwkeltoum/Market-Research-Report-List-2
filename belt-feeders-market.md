On the other hand, groove type belt feeders feature a contoured belt with grooves to assist in material flow. Both types offer efficient and reliable feeding of materials, with flat belt feeders being suitable for a wide range of applications and groove type belt feeders being ideal for handling more challenging materials.</p></p>
