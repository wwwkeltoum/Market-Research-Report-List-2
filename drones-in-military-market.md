<p><p>Drones in the military market can be categorized into three main types: Radio Remote Control, Automatic Program Control, and Integrated Control. Radio Remote Control drones are operated by a human operator using a remote control device. Automatic Program Control drones are pre-programmed to follow a specific flight path or perform certain tasks autonomously. Integrated Control drones combine both manual and automatic control capabilities, allowing for flexibility in operation. Each type of drone offers unique advantages and capabilities for military applications.</p></p>
