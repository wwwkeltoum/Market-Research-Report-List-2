<p><p>Regenerative blowers are classified into different types based on the number of stages they contain. Single-stage regenerative blowers utilize a single impeller to generate airflow, making them suitable for applications requiring medium pressure and flow rates. Two-stage regenerative blowers feature two impellers for increased pressure capabilities. Three-stage regenerative blowers have three impellers, providing even higher pressure levels. Besides these, there are other variations of regenerative blowers available in the market with unique designs and performance characteristics to cater to specific application requirements.</p></p>
