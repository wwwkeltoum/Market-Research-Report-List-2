<p><p>Ethylene and propylene cracking catalyst market types include CHP (conventional hydroprocessing), CRP (catalytic reforming process), CIP (catalytic isomerization process), MMC (metallocene catalysts), and others. CHP involves the use of traditional methods to process hydrocarbons for cracking. CRP utilizes catalysts to reform molecules and create high-octane products. CIP focuses on isomerizing molecules to improve product quality. MMC uses metallocene catalysts to enhance performance. Other market types may include innovative or specialized catalyst processes for ethylene and propylene cracking.</p></p>
