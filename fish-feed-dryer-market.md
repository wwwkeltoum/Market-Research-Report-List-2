<p><p>Fish feed dryers come in two main types: sunken type and floating type. Sunken type fish feed dryers are placed on the bottom of the drying chamber, allowing for better heat transfer and even drying of the feed. On the other hand, floating type fish feed dryers are suspended in the drying chamber, which can be advantageous for certain types of feed. Both types of dryers have their own benefits and can cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
