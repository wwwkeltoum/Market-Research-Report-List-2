<p><p>Fruit and vegetable ingredients market encompasses various types, including concentrates, pastes and purees, pieces and powders, and NFC (not-from-concentrate) juices. Concentrates are processed forms with reduced liquid content, while pastes and purees retain more of the original texture and consistency. Pieces and powders are dehydrated forms that are easy to store and use. NFC juices are made directly from fresh fruits without any added sugars or preservatives. Each type offers unique qualities and benefits for different food and beverage applications.</p></p>
