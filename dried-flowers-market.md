<p><p>Dried Flowers Market Types encompass various methods of preserving flowers for decorative and commercial purposes. Air-Drying Flowers involves hanging flowers upside down to dry naturally. Press-Drying Flowers involves pressing flowers between absorbent paper. Embedded-Drying Flowers involves embedding flowers in silica gel. Oven-Drying Flowers is done by placing flowers in a low-heat oven. Glycerine-Drying Flowers involves soaking flowers in a glycerine solution. Freeze-Drying Flowers preserves flowers by freezing them in a vacuum. Each method offers unique benefits and results in different textures and appearances.</p></p>
