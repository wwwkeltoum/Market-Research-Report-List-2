<p><p>The automotive loudspeaker market consists of two main types: air horn and electric horn. Air horns emit sound through a pressurized air system, producing a loud and distinctive noise. Electric horns, on the other hand, use an electrical current to create sound vibrations. Both types of horns are commonly used in vehicles for safety purposes, alerting pedestrians and other drivers to a vehicle's presence. The choice between air horn and electric horn depends on factors such as desired sound volume, tone, and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
