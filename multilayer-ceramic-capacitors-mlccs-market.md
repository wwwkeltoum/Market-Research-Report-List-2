<p><strong>The Multilayer Ceramic Capacitors (MLCCs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Multilayer Ceramic Capacitors (MLCCs) are categorized based on their temperature stability and dielectric constant. X7R and X5R are commonly used for general purpose applications with X7R offering better temperature stability. C0G (NP0) MLCCs have the lowest dielectric constant and are ideal for high-frequency applications. Y5V MLCCs have the highest dielectric constant but are less stable over temperature. Other types of MLCCs include Z5U and X8R, each with unique characteristics suited for specific applications.</p></p>
