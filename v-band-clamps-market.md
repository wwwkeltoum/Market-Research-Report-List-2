<p><p>V-Band Clamps are a type of mechanical fastener used to join flanged connections, typically in automotive and industrial applications. They consist of a stainless steel band with a V-shaped profile, a clamp assembly with a bridge piece and a nut, and a retaining ring. V-Band Clamps offer quick and easy installation without the need for special tools, making them popular in exhaust systems, turbochargers, intercoolers, and other high-temperature and high-pressure applications.</p><p>The V-Band Clamps Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period.
