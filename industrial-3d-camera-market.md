<p><p>Industrial 3D cameras are essential tools for capturing detailed images and measurements in various industrial applications. Area scan cameras are suitable for capturing high-quality images of stationary objects within a specific area, making them ideal for inspection and quality control tasks. On the other hand, line scan cameras are designed to capture images of moving objects across a continuous line, making them ideal for applications such as web inspection and surface inspection. Both types of cameras play a crucial role in improving efficiency and accuracy in industrial processes.</p></p>
