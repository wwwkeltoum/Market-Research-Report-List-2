<p><p>Vertical decanter centrifuges are designed with a vertical rotating bowl, allowing for efficient separation of liquids and solids. They are commonly used in industries such as wastewater treatment and food processing. On the other hand, horizontal decanter centrifuges have a horizontal rotating bowl, ideal for continuous separation of two liquids with different densities. They are often found in industries like oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals. Both types of decanter centrifuges offer high productivity, reliable performance, and low maintenance requirements.</p></p>
