<p><p>CoPET/PET Fiber Market Types refer to the classification of fibers based on their melting points. Fibers with a melting point ≤130 ℃ are suitable for applications where low heat resistance is required, such as in nonwoven fabrics, disposable textiles, and packaging materials. On the other hand, fibers with a melting point >130 ℃ are more commonly used in high-temperature applications such as in automotive textiles, geotextiles, and protective clothing. Understanding these market types helps manufacturers choose the right fiber for specific end-uses.</p></p>
