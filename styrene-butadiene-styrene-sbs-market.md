<p><p>Styrene Butadiene Styrene (SBS) is a type of synthetic rubber with various industrial applications. The market for SBS is divided into two main types based on the method of production - oil-extended SBS and non-oil-extended SBS. Oil-extended SBS is produced by blending SBS with oil, which enhances its flexibility and elasticity, making it suitable for applications in industries like construction and automotive.
