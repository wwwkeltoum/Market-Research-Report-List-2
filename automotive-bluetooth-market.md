<p><ul><li>Open HMI Connection</li><li>Proprietary HMI Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Bluetooth market types include Open HMI Connection and Proprietary HMI Connection. Open HMI Connection allows for interoperability between different devices and systems, enabling seamless communication and integration. On the other hand, Proprietary HMI Connection restricts communication to specific devices or systems designed by the same manufacturer, limiting compatibility with other products. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, catering to different user preferences and needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
