<p><p>Vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V) communication utilizes Cellular Based Technology to enable cars to exchange information wirelessly, aiding in traffic safety and efficiency. This type of communication relies on cell towers to transmit data between vehicles. On the other hand, Dedicated Short-Range Communication (DSRC) market uses a specific frequency band for direct communication between vehicles, offering lower latency and increased security compared to Cellular Based Technology. Both markets aim to enhance road safety and traffic management through real-time data sharing among vehicles.</p></p>
