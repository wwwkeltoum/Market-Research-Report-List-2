<p><p>Ambulatory Cardiac Monitoring Systems are used to continuously monitor a patient's heart activity outside of a clinical setting. The main types of ambulatory cardiac monitoring systems include Holter Monitors, which record heart activity over 24-48 hours, Event Monitors, which are wearable devices that record heart activity when activated by the patient, Mobile Cardiac Telemetry, which provides continuous monitoring and transmission of heart data, and other emerging markets such as patch monitors and implantable cardiac monitors. These systems are used for diagnosing and monitoring various cardiac conditions.</p></p>
