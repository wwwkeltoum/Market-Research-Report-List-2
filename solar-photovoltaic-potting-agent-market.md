<p><p>Solar photovoltaic potting agents are used to encapsulate solar panels to protect them from environmental factors. One-component potting agents are ready-to-use materials that require no mixing, while dual-component potting agents consist of two separate components that must be mixed before use. One-component potting agents are preferred for their simplicity and ease of use, while dual-component potting agents offer more control over the curing process and can be customized for specific applications. Both types play a key role in ensuring the longevity and performance of solar panels.</p></p>
