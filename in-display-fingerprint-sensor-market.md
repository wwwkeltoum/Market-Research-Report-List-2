<p><p>In the In-Display Fingerprint Sensor market, there are mainly three types: Optical, Ultrasonic, and Capacitive sensors. Optical sensors use light to capture a 2D image of the fingerprint, Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to create a 3D image, and Capacitive sensors use electrical current to map the fingerprint. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of accuracy, speed, security, and reliability, catering to different consumer needs and preferences within the market.</p></p>
