<p><strong>The Cold Bend Testing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-Point Bend</li><li>4-Point Bend</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold Bend Testing Machine market can be classified into two main types: 3-Point Bend and 4-Point Bend. 3-Point Bend testing involves applying force on a specimen at two points while supporting it at a third point in the middle. This method is commonly used for determining the flexibility and elongation properties of materials. On the other hand, 4-Point Bend testing applies force at two outer points and supports the specimen at two inner points. This method provides more accurate and consistent results for measuring the strength and stiffness of materials.</p></p>
