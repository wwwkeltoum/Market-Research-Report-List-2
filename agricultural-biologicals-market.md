<p><p>The Agricultural Biologicals Market consists of three main types of products: Biopesticides, Biostimulants, and Biofertilizers. Biopesticides are natural alternatives to chemical pesticides made from living organisms, such as bacteria or fungi, that control plant pests. Biostimulants are substances that enhance plant growth and crop yield by stimulating natural processes. Biofertilizers are organic fertilizers containing living microorganisms that improve soil fertility and plant nutrient uptake. These products offer sustainable and environmentally friendly solutions for agriculture.</p></p>
