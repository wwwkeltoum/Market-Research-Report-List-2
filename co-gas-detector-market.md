<p><p>CO gas detectors are classified into two main types: wall/fixed detectors and portable detectors. Wall/fixed detectors are permanently installed in specific locations to continuously monitor carbon monoxide levels. They are commonly used in homes, offices, and industrial settings. Portable detectors, on the other hand, are handheld devices that can be carried around to different locations for on-the-go monitoring. They are typically used by first responders, industrial workers, and individuals looking to test CO levels in various settings. Both types of detectors play a crucial role in ensuring safety and preventing CO poisoning.</p></p>
