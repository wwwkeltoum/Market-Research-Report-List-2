<p><p>Farm insurance is a type of insurance coverage designed to protect farmers against financial losses resulting from unexpected events such as natural disasters, crop failures, equipment breakdowns, or even liability issues. Overall, the Farm Insurance Market is expected to continue growing as farmers recognize the value of protecting their livelihoods through insurance coverage.</p></p>
