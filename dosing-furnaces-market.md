<p><ul><li>Electrical Heating System</li><li>Electro-pneumatic Dose System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dosing furnaces are available in two main types: Electrical Heating System and Electro-pneumatic Dose System. The Electrical Heating System uses electricity to generate heat for the dosing furnace, allowing for precise control of temperature. On the other hand, the Electro-pneumatic Dose System utilizes a combination of electricity and pneumatic pressure to accurately control the dosing process. Both systems offer efficient and reliable dosing solutions for various industrial applications.</p></p>
