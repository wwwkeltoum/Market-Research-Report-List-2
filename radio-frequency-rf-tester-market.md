<p><p>Radio Frequency RF testers come in four main types: Network, Spectrum, Oscilloscope, and Signal Generator. Network testers are used to test the performance of networks and communication systems. Spectrum testers are used to measure the frequency spectrum of RF signals. Oscilloscope testers are used to display and analyze the waveform of RF signals. Signal Generator testers are used to generate RF signals for testing purposes. Each type of RF tester has its own specific functions and applications in the RF testing market.</p></p>
