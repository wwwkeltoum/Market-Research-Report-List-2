<p><strong>The Vegetated Green Roof Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extensive (Shallow Depth) Modules</li><li>Semi-Intensive (Intermediate Depth) Modules</li><li>Intensive (Maximum Depth) Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vegetated Green Roofs are categorized into three market types based on their depth and level of maintenance required. Extensive modules have shallow depth, require minimal maintenance, and are primarily used for environmental benefits like stormwater management. Semi-Intensive modules have an intermediate depth, allowing for a wider variety of plants to be grown and moderate maintenance. Intensive modules have the maximum depth, allowing for a diverse range of plants and even outdoor living spaces, but require more maintenance and cost.</p></p>
