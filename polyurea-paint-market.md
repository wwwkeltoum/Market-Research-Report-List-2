<p><ul><li>Pure Polyurea Coating</li><li>Hybrid Polyurea Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurea paint market consists of two main types: Pure Polyurea Coating and Hybrid Polyurea Coating. Pure Polyurea Coating is made purely of polyurea components, offering superior durability, chemical resistance, and crack bridging abilities. On the other hand, Hybrid Polyurea Coating is a combination of polyurea with other coatings like polyurethane or epoxies to enhance certain properties such as UV stability, adhesion, or flexibility. Both types cater to different needs and applications in industries such as construction, automotive, and marine.</p></p>
