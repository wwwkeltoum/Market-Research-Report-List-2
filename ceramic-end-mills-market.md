<p><p>Ceramic end mills are cutting tools used in various industries for machining applications. In terms of material composition, they are typically categorized into three main types based on the metal used in their manufacturing - iron-based, nickel-based, and cobalt-based. Iron-based ceramic end mills are known for their high strength and toughness, making them suitable for general purpose machining. Nickel-based ones offer excellent wear resistance and high-temperature properties, while cobalt-based ceramic end mills are preferred for their superior heat resistance and hardness, making them ideal for hard materials.</p></p>
