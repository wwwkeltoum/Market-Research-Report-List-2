<p><p>Bitumen additives market can be categorized into four main types: Emulsifiers, which help in creating stable emulsions for cold mix applications; Polymeric modifiers, which enhance the performance and durability of bitumen in various applications; Anti-strip and adhesion promoters, which improve the adhesion between bitumen and aggregates; and Other additives such as anti-oxidants, anti-foaming agents, and rejuvenators, which provide additional functionalities to bitumen based on specific application requirements. Each of these types plays a crucial role in enhancing the properties and performance of bitumen in various construction applications.</p></p>
