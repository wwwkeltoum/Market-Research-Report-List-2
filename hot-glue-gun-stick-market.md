<p><p>The Hot Glue Gun Stick market can be classified into two main types: Opaque and Transparent. Opaque hot glue gun sticks are solid in color, with no see-through qualities. They are commonly used for crafts and projects where a solid, opaque bond is needed. On the other hand, Transparent hot glue gun sticks are clear and allow for visibility through the bond. These types are preferred for applications where a seamless and invisible bond is required, such as in glass or plastic projects.</p></p>
