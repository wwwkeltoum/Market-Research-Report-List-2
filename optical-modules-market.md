<p><p>Optical modules are electronic devices used in fiber optic communication networks to transmit and receive data. The market types include Optical Receiver Modules, which receive optical signals and convert them into electrical signals; Optical Transmitter Modules, which convert electrical signals into optical signals for transmission; Optical Transceiver Modules, which can both transmit and receive signals; and Optical Transponder Modules, which can convert and amplify optical signals for long-distance transmission. These modules are essential components in high-speed communication networks.</p></p>
