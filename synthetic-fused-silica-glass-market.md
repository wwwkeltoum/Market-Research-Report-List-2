<p><strong>The Synthetic Fused Silica Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CVD Process</li><li>Plasma Deposition Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Synthetic Fused Silica Glass market includes two main types: CVD Process and Plasma Deposition Process. The CVD Process involves creating a thin film of silica glass on a substrate through chemical vapor deposition, resulting in high-purity and precise optical properties. On the other hand, the Plasma Deposition Process uses plasma to deposit silica glass layer by layer, offering improved control over thickness and uniformity. Both processes cater to different applications and industries within the market for synthetic fused silica glass.</p></p>
