<p><strong>The Well-control Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brine</li><li>Polymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Well-control fluid market is divided into two main types: brine and polymer-based fluids. Brine fluids are typically made of saltwater and are commonly used for well control and drilling operations. They are cost-effective and have good stability properties. On the other hand, polymer-based fluids are made of synthetic polymers and offer improved viscosity and lubricity compared to brine fluids. They also provide better hole cleaning capabilities and thermal stability, making them suitable for more challenging drilling environments. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining well control during drilling operations.</p></p>
