<p><p>Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillators (TCXOs) are available in two main mounting types: Surface Mount and Thru-hole Mounting. Surface Mount TCXOs are designed to be mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, saving space and providing efficient heat dissipation. Thru-hole Mount TCXOs require holes to be drilled on the circuit board for installation. Both mounting types are used in various electronic applications where precise frequency and temperature stability are required, such as telecommunications, aerospace, and military equipment.</p></p>
