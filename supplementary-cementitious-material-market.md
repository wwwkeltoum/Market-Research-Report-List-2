<p><p>The Supplementary Cementitious Material (SCM) market can be classified into two main types: solid and powder. Solid SCM products are typically in the form of granules or pellets, while powder SCM products are finely ground materials. Solid SCMs are often easier to handle and transport, while powder SCMs may have better dispersibility in the mix. Both types of SCMs are commonly used in the construction industry to improve the performance and sustainability of concrete mixes.</p></p>
