<p><p>Glass block markets can be divided into two main types based on the type of glass used: tempered glass block and annealed glass block. Tempered glass block is heat-treated to make it stronger and more durable, making it ideal for applications where safety and security are important. Annealed glass block, on the other hand, is untreated and more prone to breaking, but is typically more affordable. Both types of glass block have their own advantages and are used in different applications depending on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
