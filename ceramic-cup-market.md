<p><p>The Ceramic Cup Market can be categorized into three types based on firing temperature: Low (700~900°C), Medium (1000~1200°C), and High (≥1200°C). Low firing temperature ceramic cups are more fragile and less durable, suitable for decorative purposes. Medium firing temperature cups are more durable and commonly used for everyday drinking. High firing temperature cups are the most durable and heat-resistant, often used for special purposes like holding hot beverages or in industrial settings. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
