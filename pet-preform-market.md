<p><p>The PET Preform market can be divided into two main types based on the neck finish of the preforms. Non-Crystallized Neck Finish Preforms are preforms that have not undergone the process of crystallization at the neck finish. On the other hand, Crystallized Neck Finish Preforms have undergone crystallization at the neck finish. Both types of preforms have their own advantages and are used in various applications within the packaging industry. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the packaging process.</p></p>
