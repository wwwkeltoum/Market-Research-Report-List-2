<p><p>Learning Management System (LMS) software is available in two main market types: Cloud-based and web-based. Cloud-based LMS are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the platform over the internet. This type offers flexibility, scalability, and automatic updates without the need for on-site servers. Meanwhile, web-based LMS are accessed through a web browser and are typically installed on local servers. This type provides customization options and greater control over data. Organizations can choose the market type that best fits their needs and requirements.</p></p>
