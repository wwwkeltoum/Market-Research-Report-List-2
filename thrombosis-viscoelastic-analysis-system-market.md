TEG measures the viscoelastic properties of blood clot formation, providing a comprehensive assessment of hemostasis. ROTEM is a similar technology that evaluates the clotting process in real-time. These systems are used in various clinical settings to guide hemostatic therapy in patients with bleeding disorders, surgery, trauma, and other conditions where monitoring coagulation is critical for patient outcomes.</p></p>
