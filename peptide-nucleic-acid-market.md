<p><p>Peptide Nucleic Acid (PNA) is a type of synthetic nucleic acid analog that has unique properties making it highly valuable in various applications. PNA can be classified into two types based on their structure: Double Chain PNA and Single Chain PNA. Double Chain PNAs have two strands of PNA connected by a peptide linker, while Single Chain PNAs consist of a single PNA molecule. Both types of PNAs have their own distinct advantages and applications in research, diagnostics, and therapeutics.</p></p>
