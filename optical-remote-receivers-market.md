<p><strong>The Optical Remote Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Receiving Type</li><li>Passive Receiving Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical remote receivers can be classified into two main types: active receiving type and passive receiving type. Active receiving type receivers require external power to operate and typically offer higher sensitivity and better signal quality. On the other hand, passive receiving type receivers do not require external power and rely on natural sources of light for operation. Passive receivers are often used in low-power applications where energy efficiency is critical. Both types of receivers play a crucial role in various optical communication systems.</p></p>
