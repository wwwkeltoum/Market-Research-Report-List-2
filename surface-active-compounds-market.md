<p><p>Surface-active compounds market can be classified into four main types based on their ionic charge: anionic surfactants, non-ionic surfactants, cationic surfactants, and amphoteric surfactants. Anionic surfactants have a negative charge and are commonly used in household detergents. Non-ionic surfactants have no charge and are often used in personal care products. Cationic surfactants have a positive charge and are typically found in fabric softeners. Amphoteric surfactants have both positive and negative charges, making them versatile for various applications in industries such as agriculture and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
