<p><p>Flexible packaging laminating adhesives come in three main types: water based, solvent based, and solvent less. Water based adhesives are environmentally friendly and offer good heat resistance. Solvent based adhesives provide high bond strength and fast drying times, but may have negative health and environmental impacts. Solvent less adhesives offer high bond strength without the use of harmful solvents, making them a popular choice for food packaging applications. Each type has its own advantages and considerations, catering to different needs in the flexible packaging industry.</p></p>
