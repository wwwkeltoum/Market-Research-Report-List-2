<p><strong>The Time of Flight ToF Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RF-Modulated Light Sources</li><li>Range Gated Imagers</li><li>Direct Time-Of-Flight Imagers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Time of Flight (ToF) sensors use various methods to measure the distance between the sensor and an object. RF-Modulated Light Sources use radio frequencies to determine the time taken for light to travel to an object and back. Range Gated Imagers use pulsed light and a high-speed camera to measure distance. Direct Time-Of-Flight Imagers measure distance by calculating the time taken for light to reflect off an object. These technologies cater to different applications and industries within the ToF sensor market.</p></p>
