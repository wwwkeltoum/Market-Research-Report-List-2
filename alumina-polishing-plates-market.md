<p><p>Alumina polishing plates are available in four different types based on their hardness level: Very Soft Type, Soft Type, Medium Type, and Hard Type. Very Soft Type plates are gentle on delicate surfaces and are ideal for finishing applications. Soft Type plates provide a balance between gentleness and abrasiveness, suitable for general polishing tasks. Medium Type plates offer moderate abrasiveness, while Hard Type plates are highly abrasive and best suited for heavy-duty polishing requirements. Customers can choose the type based on the specific needs of their polishing projects.</p></p>
